## 2. How They Position Themselves

### Common Positioning Language (Copy These Patterns)
- **Outcome-first**: "Reduce costs and increase revenue" (AI REV)
- **Anti-PowerPoint**: "We don't just consult -- we implement" (Addepto) / "Not recommendations gathering dust"
- **Speed**: "AI transformation within 30 days" (Turing)
- **Custom**: "Bespoke" / "tailor-made" / "built for your workflows"
- **ROI-focused**: "Systems that pay for themselves" (Master of Code)
- **Domain expertise**: "20+ industries" / vertical-specific language

### Positioning Formula (Hero Section)
```
[What we do] + [Who it's for] + [Differentiator/Outcome]
```
**Best example**: "We Empower Businesses to Reduce Costs and Increase Revenue" (AI REV)

### Proof Points That Work
1. **Quantified outcomes**: "92 min/week saved per employee" (Honeywell/Copilot case)
2. **Time compression**: "12 weeks reduced to 10 minutes" (Novo Nordisk clinical reports)
3. **Cost reduction %**: "25% reduction in customer service costs" (AmEx chatbot)
4. **Client logos**: Display recognizable brands in a logo bar below the hero
5. **Badge-style social proof**: "Trusted by 9,000 teams"

### What to AVOID
- Vague "AI-powered solutions" without specifics
- Technology-first messaging (no one cares you use RAG; they care you cut costs 30%)
- Generic stock photos of robots/brains

## 3. Pricing Models

### The Pricing Landscape (US Market, 2026)

| Engagement Type | Freelance | Boutique Agency | Enterprise Firm |
|----------------|-----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Day Rate | $600-$1,200 | $1,500-$2,500 | $2,500-$3,000+ |
| Hourly Rate | $100-$300 | $150-$400 | Rarely offered |
| Strategy Project | $10K-$30K | $30K-$80K | $80K-$200K |
| Proof-of-Concept | $20K-$60K | $50K-$150K | $150K-$500K |
| Full AI Solution | $50K-$150K | $150K-$500K+ | $500K-$2M+ |
| Monthly Retainer | $3K-$10K/mo | $10K-$30K+/mo | $30K-$100K+/mo |

### Recommended Pricing Model for Personal Intelligence

**Hybrid model (most successful in 2026):**
1. **Paid Discovery Phase** ($2,500-$5,000): Audit client's workflows, identify AI opportunities, deliver roadmap. Many close at 60%+ conversion to implementation.
2. **Project-Based Setup** ($5,000-$50,000): Build the custom AI system
3. **Monthly Retainer** ($2,000-$8,000/mo): Ongoing maintenance, optimization, support
4. **Performance Bonus** (optional): 10-20% of documented cost savings in Year 1

**Value-Based Pricing Rule**: Price at 30-50% of the total cost of the manual process your AI replaces. If client spends $100K/year on SDRs and your AI handles 70%, price at $25K-$35K/year.

### Retainer Tiers (Copy This Structure)
| Tier | Hours/Month | Price/Month | Includes |
|------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| Advisory | 5-10 hrs | $2,000-$5,000 | Strategy calls, prompt tuning, monitoring |
| Standard | 10-25 hrs | $5,000-$15,000 | Active optimization, new feature builds |
| Partnership | 25+ hrs | $15,000-$50,000 | Embedded AI team, full lifecycle support |

### Greek Market Adjustment
Given Greece's lower wage structure (avg tech salary ~EUR 1,597/mo), Personal Intelligence can undercut US firms by 40-60% while maintaining 70-80% margins. Target:
- Discovery: EUR 1,500-3,000

For US/EU clients serviced remotely, maintain US-adjacent pricing (20-30% discount to US rates).

## 4. Sales Funnels & Client Acquisition

### How Top AI Agencies Acquire Clients

**Channel Mix (ranked by effectiveness in 2026):**

1. **Content Marketing / Thought Leadership** (40% of pipeline for boutiques)
   - LinkedIn posts showing real AI implementations
   - Case study blog posts with quantified ROI
   - "AI audit" lead magnets (free mini-assessment)
   - YouTube/short-form video demos of systems working
   - Newsletter with AI insights for specific verticals

2. **Referrals & Partnerships** (30%)
   - Existing client referrals (highest close rate: 50-70%)
   - Technology partner referrals (e.g., recommended by Make.com, n8n)
   - Industry association partnerships
   - Co-selling with complementary agencies (web dev, marketing)

3. **Cold Outreach** (15%)
   - AI-personalized cold email sequences
   - LinkedIn DM outreach to decision-makers
   - "Free AI audit" as door opener
   - Industry events and conferences

4. **Paid Advertising** (10%)
   - Google Ads targeting "AI consulting [industry]"
   - LinkedIn Ads to C-suite in target verticals
   - Retargeting visitors who downloaded lead magnets

5. **Marketplace Listings** (5%)
   - Clutch.co, GoodFirms, Upwork Pro for credibility
   - G2 reviews (if you productize any tools)

### The Winning Funnel Structure
```
Awareness: Thought leadership content + SEO
    |
Interest: Free AI assessment / audit tool on website
    |
Consideration: Paid discovery phase ($2,500-$5,000)
    |
Decision: Custom proposal with ROI projections
    |
Retention: Monthly retainer + quarterly business reviews
```

### Sales Language That Converts
- "What does [manual process X] cost you per month?"
- "Here's what that looks like automated"
- "We'll build a proof-of-concept in 2 weeks"
- "You only pay the retainer once the system is live"

## 6. The "AI Agency" / "AI Automation Agency" Trend

### What It Is
A new breed of agency (2024-2026 explosion) that builds AI automations for SMBs using platforms like Make.com, n8n, Voiceflow, Botpress. Lower-touch than traditional consulting; more productized.

### Market Size
- AI agents market: ~$12-15B (2025) growing to $80-100B (2030)
- 72% of US small businesses use at least one AI tool (up from 48% in 2024)
- 67% of SMBs using AI automation saw 20%+ revenue growth

### Typical AAA (AI Automation Agency) Profile
- **Team size**: 1-10 people
- **Revenue**: $100K-$2M/year
- **Margins**: 70-85%
- **Average client**: $2,000-$8,000/mo retainer
- **Stack**: Make.com / n8n + Voiceflow/Botpress + OpenAI/Anthropic API + CRM integration
- **Delivery**: Remote, often global clients

### Key Trends
1. **Cost compression**: What cost $500-1,000/mo in 2024 now runs $50-200/mo
2. **Voice AI explosion**: $0.07-0.12/min vs. $1.50-3.00/min for human answering service
3. **White-labeling**: Agencies reselling agent-building platforms under own brand
4. **Vertical specialization**: Niche = premium pricing (2-3x generalist rates)
5. **Paid discovery**: Charging $2,500-$5,000 upfront for workflow audits before committing

### How Personal Intelligence Should Position vs. AAAs
Personal Intelligence is NOT an "AI automation agency" (commodity). It is a **bespoke AI systems consultancy** that builds custom, enterprise-grade solutions. The differentiation:
- AAA = assembles no-code tools; PI = engineers custom systems
- AAA = template-based; PI = architecture-first
- AAA = $2K-$8K/mo; PI = $5K-$30K/mo (for more complex, higher-value work)

## 7. Best Landing Pages & What Makes Them Convert

### Website Structure That Converts (Copy This)

```
HERO SECTION
  - Clear headline (6-10 words): outcome-focused, not feature-focused
  - Subheadline: who you help + how
  - Primary CTA: "Book a Free AI Assessment" / "Schedule a Strategy Call"
  - Secondary CTA: "See Our Work" / "View Case Studies"
  - Social proof: client logos or "Trusted by X companies"

PROOF BAR
  - 3-4 key metrics: "47% avg cost reduction" | "90-day deployment" | "24/7 AI agents"

SERVICES SECTION
  - 3-4 core offerings with icons and 2-line descriptions
  - Each links to a detailed service page

CASE STUDIES (Critical)
  - 2-3 highlighted case studies with:
    - Client industry
    - Problem
    - Solution built
    - Quantified result ("Reduced support costs by 62%")

HOW WE WORK
  - 3-4 step process: Discovery > Design > Build > Optimize
  - Sets expectations, reduces friction

TESTIMONIALS
  - Video preferred; text with photo + name + title acceptable
  - If early-stage: advisor endorsements, beta user feedback

FAQ SECTION
  - Address objections: "How long does implementation take?"
  - "What if it doesn't work?" "How do you handle our data?"

FINAL CTA
  - Repeat the primary CTA
  - Add urgency or scarcity if authentic
```
